Lara Minkus, PhD

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Flensburg. I received my PhD in Sociology from the Bremen International Graduate School of Social Sciences (BIGSSS). I am interested in Gender, Families, Social Inequality and public opinion dynamics.
My work has been published in Perspectives on Politics, Social Politics, and EPJ Data Science among others.
